Monster Hunter World

Monster Hunter World is an action role plating game where players become a monster hunter that exploring the world to defet creatures for money and it is part of the story too. The setting of the game is a place call New Workd which full of monster that is ready to be hunted. The game allows you to track, capture, and kill the monsters using any weapon that you choose.

The game is known for its combat system and the variety of weapons which give you a different playstyle on every wepons. Players also need to craft armor and consumable from the materials that they collected from slaying monsters. This game also allow players to play COOP mode so they can enjoy the game with heir friends. This game is a fun game to play and if you want a bit of challenges in your game, this might be the one for you.
