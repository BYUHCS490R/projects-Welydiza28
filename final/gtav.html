Grand Theft Auto V

Grand Theft Auto V or GTA V is an open world action adventure game that set in the state of San Andreas with three main characters (Michael, Franklin, and Trevor). These three playable characters always involved in heists. Players can freely use and switch between these three characters on missions or explorations around the map.

The game is known for its big open world map with detailed aspects and with lots of activities that allow all players to do anything. GTA V also has an online option that allow players to make their own character and play with their friends together. Overall, GTA V is very popular for its detailed map, storyline, freedom of gameplay, and realistic feelings when playing on it. This is my number one game all the time.
