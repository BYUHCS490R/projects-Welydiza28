What is Brawlstar?

Brawlstars is a multiplayer online game. It was released on 12 December 2018. It's a battle arena and hero shooter game. The developer of this game is Supercell the one that developed Clash of CLans.

Me personally play this game from it first came. You can play this game on iOS or Android. Right now, they made a competition and make this game an e-sports that you can compete with other players and teams.
